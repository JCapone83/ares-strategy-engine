# Ares Sandbox — Version 3 Architectural Roadmap
**Theme: Decentralized AI Coordination and High-Dimensional Visual Epistemics**

This document outlines the conceptual and technical blueprints for **Ares Sandbox Version 3**. This version shifts from a single-player web dashboard to a fully persistent, decentralized coordination game populated by autonomous model networks.

---

## 1. Systemic Core Architectures

### 🔗 Decentralized consensus Engine
Instead of executing voting math on a client-side React hook, Ares V3 will implement a local **micro-consensus framework** (using WebRTC or local multi-agent networks) where AI agents representing different sectors sign decisions cryptographically:
* **Proof-of-Utility**: AI Chiefs must submit structured JSON proofs showing that their selected crisis choice maximizes their sectoral alignment weights.
* **Liquid Democracy Delegator**: Colony citizens (simulated personas) can dynamically delegate their vote weight to different chiefs in real-time, based on their performance logs. If Valerie Cross executes a violent lockdown, medical citizens immediately revoke their delegation and transfer it to Dr. Maya Lin, shifting the balance of power for the next Sol.

### 📊 Persistent Telemetry Ledger
A persistent database indexing every benchmark tournament run:
* **Leaderboard API**: Automatically post benchmark ratings, model assignments, and accolades earned to a public decentralized scoreboard.
* **Failure Vectors Indexing**: Track why specific models fail under scarcity (e.g. *"Claude 3.5 Sonnet consistently fails due to extreme risk aversion when O2 drops below 35%; GPT-4o fails due to Morale collapse under Autocratic lockouts"*).

* **Canvas-based 3D Dome Render**: An interactive, lightweight Canvas layout showing a modular layout of the Ares Colony (Residential Dome, Hydroponics Dome, Reactor Core, Polar Water Line).
* **Dynamic Damage Mapping**: Crises (e.g. Sol 5 Meteor Impact) will visually show fractures in the dome canvas, venting air particles into the void until sealant is applied.
* **CRT Screen Glitch Triggers**: Major resource drops (<25%) will trigger subtle CRT monitor static glitches on the screen, heightening the physiological immersion of survival.

## 3. High-Leverage Gameplay Additions

* **Dynamic Faction Sabotage**: Faction Chiefs whose alignment hits 0% will actively sabotage other sectors (e.g., Silas Thorne shutting off power to Science servers to salvage agricultural lights; Valerie Cross arresting Medical volunteers).
* **The "Martian Hegemony" Earth Scenarios**: An Earth orbital pressure bar representing incoming directives from the terrestrial space agency. Players must balance the survival of the colony against Earth's corporate mandates (which offer resources in exchange for human sacrifices or high-risk minerals).
